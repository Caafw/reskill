**AI-powered course outline generator — Centre for Applied AI and Future of Work**

Generate a structured course outline on any subject in minutes. Built for learners, instructors, and instructional designers. Free to explore. Full course content delivered through CAAFW.

## What it does

A single-file interactive tool that walks you through five steps:

1. **Subject** — choose from sample topics or request your own
2. **Your role** — Learner, Instructor, or Instructional Designer
3. **Level** — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
4. **Format** — Full text, structured points, or presenter notes
5. **Generate** — AI produces a complete course outline: modules, lessons, outcomes, assessments

The outline is yours to keep, share, and use for planning.

**Full course content** — lesson text, worked examples, assessments, and deep course generation — is delivered through CAAFW Re/Skill.
